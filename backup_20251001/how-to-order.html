How to Order

Ordering from SpurMount Wholesale is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to place your order.

1

Browse Our Products

Visit our Products page to see our wide range of wholesale items. Each product has detailed information including pricing and available quantities.

2

Select Your Items

Click on the "Order Now" button for each product you wish to purchase. This will open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing the product details.

Alternatively, you can note down the product names and quantities and send us a direct WhatsApp message.

3

Send Your Order

Send your order via WhatsApp to +254 712 345 678. Please include:

  • Your name
  • Delivery address
  • List of items and quantities
  • Preferred delivery date and time (if any)
4

Order Confirmation

Our team will review your order and send you a confirmation with the total amount including any applicable delivery charges. We'll also provide you with payment instructions.

5

Make Payment

Make payment via M-Pesa using our Paybill number: 123456. Use your business name as the account number. After payment, send us the transaction details for verification.

For more payment options, visit our Payment page.

6

Delivery

Once payment is confirmed, we'll process your order for delivery. You'll receive a notification with tracking information. For more details about our delivery service, visit our Delivery page.
