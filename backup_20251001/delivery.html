Delivery FAQs

How can I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can use this to track your order on our website or contact our customer service for updates.
What are your delivery hours?
We deliver Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Deliveries are not made on Sundays and public holidays.
What if I'm not available to receive my delivery?
If you're not available, our delivery team will attempt to contact you to reschedule. Alternatively, you can authorize someone else to receive the order on your behalf.
Do you offer same-day delivery?
Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12:00 PM within Nairobi, subject to product availability and delivery location. Additional charges may apply.
