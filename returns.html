At SpurMount Wholesale, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. Please review our return and refund policy below.

Policy at a Glance

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from the delivery date to initiate a return for eligible items.

Original Condition

Items must be in their original packaging and condition, unused and unopened.

Return Shipping

Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.

1. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In its original packaging
  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Returned within 7 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods (unless spoiled or damaged)
  • Custom or special order items
  • Items not in their original condition
  • Items that are damaged due to customer misuse

2. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us at returns@spurmount.co.ke or call +254 700 000000 within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and details about the product you would like to return
  3. Our customer service team will guide you through the return process
  4. Once approved, you will receive a Return Authorization Number (RMA)
  5. Package the item securely with the RMA number clearly visible
  6. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping service

3. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed as follows:

3.1 Refund Methods

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days to process
  • M-Pesa: 1-2 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days

3.2 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following cases:

  • Item is returned used or damaged
  • Original packaging is missing
  • Some items from the order are missing

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@spurmount.co.ke with the following information:

  • Order number
  • Photos of the damaged/defective item
  • Description of the issue

We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

5. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

6. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error. We recommend using a trackable shipping service as we cannot be responsible for items lost in transit.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return and refund policy, please contact us:

Email: returns@spurmount.co.ke
Phone: +254 700 000000
Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM EAT

Our customer service team is here to help! Contact us for assistance with your return or if you have any questions about our policy.
