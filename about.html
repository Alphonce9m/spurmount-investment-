About SpurMount Wholesale

Your trusted partner for quality wholesale products and reliable delivery

Our Story

Founded in 2020, SpurMount Wholesale has grown from a small local supplier to a trusted name in the wholesale distribution industry. What started as a modest operation with just a handful of products has now expanded to serve businesses across Kenya with a comprehensive range of high-quality goods.

Our journey has been driven by a simple philosophy: to provide exceptional value through quality products, competitive pricing, and outstanding customer service. Over the years, we've built strong relationships with manufacturers and suppliers, allowing us to offer our customers the best products at the most competitive prices.

Our Mission

To empower businesses across Kenya by providing reliable access to high-quality wholesale products, delivered with exceptional service and competitive pricing. We are committed to building lasting partnerships that drive mutual growth and success.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted wholesale partner, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to continuously expand our product range and services to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at SpurMount Wholesale:

Quality

We source only the finest products and maintain strict quality control measures.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Their success is our success.

Reliability

We deliver on our promises with consistent, dependable service.
